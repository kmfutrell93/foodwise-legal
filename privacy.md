# FoodWise: Privacy Policy

**App name:** FoodWise: GLP-1 Meal Planner
**Effective date:** May 14, 2026
**Developer:** Kenneth Futrell
**Support:** support@foodwise.app
**Jurisdiction:** Maryland, United States

---

We built FoodWise to help people eating well on GLP-1 medications — not to collect or monetize your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and who we share it with. No surprises.

---

## What We Collect

### During Onboarding
When you set up FoodWise, we ask for information that helps us personalize your meal plans:

- GLP-1 medication type, injection day, and dose
- Side effects and food aversions
- Dietary restrictions and weekly grocery budget
- Protein goal, appetite level, and preferred check-in time
- Your name (optional)

### While You Use the App
- Symptom logs: symptoms, severity, energy level, optional notes, and timestamp
- Meal plan interactions: which plans were generated, viewed, or swapped
- Grocery list activity: items checked off
- Streak and milestone progress
- AI-generated weekly wellness reports

### Automatically
- An anonymous user ID (created by Supabase Auth — no email or password required)
- Push notification token (if you enable notifications)

### What We Don't Collect
Location, contacts, camera, photos, microphone, biometrics, or any financial account data.

---

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data for one purpose: making FoodWise work for you. Specifically:

- Generating personalized meal plans adjusted for your injection day, symptoms, and preferences
- Tracking your streaks and milestones
- Producing your weekly wellness report
- Sending the notifications you opted into

We don't use your data for advertising. We don't sell it. Period.

---

## Who We Share Data With

We work with a small number of trusted services to run the app. Here's exactly what each one receives:

| Service | Purpose | What they receive |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Database, auth, backend | All profile and log data, stored on AWS-hosted Postgres |
| **Anthropic Claude** | AI meal plan and insight generation | Medication type, injection day, symptoms, food preferences, budget — sent per request, not stored or used for training |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management | Anonymous user ID, purchase transactions, subscription status |
| **Mixpanel** | Product analytics | Anonymous user ID, in-app events (screens viewed, features used) — no health data |
| **Expo / Apple APNs** | Push notifications | Your push token, used only to deliver FoodWise notifications |

We do not share your data with anyone else.

---

## Subscriptions

FoodWise offers a free tier and FoodWise Pro (monthly and annual). All purchases are processed by Apple In-App Purchase via RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment card details.

---

## Push Notifications

If you enable notifications during onboarding, we'll send:
- A weekly wellness summary (Sundays at 6pm)
- An injection day reminder (7am on your dose day)
- A gentle re-engagement nudge if you haven't logged in 48+ hours (max once per 72 hours)

You can turn off notifications anytime in your device Settings.

---

## Your Data, Your Control

**Delete your account:** Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your profile, symptom logs, meal plans, grocery lists, streaks, milestones, weekly reports, and your Supabase Auth record — all of it, immediately.

**Mixpanel anonymization:** If you'd like your Mixpanel analytics data anonymized, email us at support@foodwise.app.

---

## Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, everything is gone immediately. No archiving, no soft deletes.
